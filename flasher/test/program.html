ID00M flasher — write-path test

Validates the flash write path. Do step 1 first (read-only) to confirm read-back works, then step 2. Chromium desktop, served over localhost, module plugged in.

Safe test procedure: pick the firmware currently on the module (build/custom/IDUM_Custom.ino.hex). Step 1 only reads. Step 2 erases & re-writes that same image, so a success leaves the module exactly as it was.
